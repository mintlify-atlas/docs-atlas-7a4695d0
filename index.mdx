Onion Browser is a free web browser for iPhone and iPad that routes all your internet traffic through the [Tor network](https://www.torproject.org/), keeping your browsing private, anonymous, and secure. It works alongside [Orbot](https://orbot.app/) or uses a built-in Tor engine — giving you control over how you connect.

## What Onion Browser does

All web traffic — including audio and video streams — is routed through Tor before leaving your device. No other app on iOS offers this level of network-layer privacy for everyday browsing.

## Quick setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Onion Browser">
    Download Onion Browser from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/onion-browser/id519296448). It's free and open source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install and start Orbot">
    Install [Orbot](https://apps.apple.com/app/orbot/id1609461599) from the App Store. Orbot provides the Tor VPN tunnel that Onion Browser uses by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Onion Browser">
    Open Onion Browser. It will detect Orbot and prompt you to start the Tor connection. Once connected, you'll see the start page and can begin browsing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your security level">
    Tap the shield icon in the toolbar to select a security level — **Gold** for maximum privacy, **Silver** for balanced usability, or **Bronze** for compatibility with most sites.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Onion Browser requires iOS 15 or later. An iPhone or iPad running iOS 15+ is needed to use the app.
</Note>
